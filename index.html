About

Tell me, where is your Maker now?
Call him. Call his wrath down upon me.
You cannot. For he does not exist.

The Elder One, Dragon Age: Inquisition

|OG| is one of the oldest guilds in Heroes of Dragon Age, from before the Guild Hall in the forums were created. In fact, the Guild Hall was created in response to the huge community developing in OG itself. Since then, we've survived through the times, laying dormant and waiting for true in-game guilds. With guild wars becoming part of the game, we are here to stay and stronger than ever, ready to face the opposition and make ourselves known.

In Dragon Age lore, the old gods were seven ancient dragons, worshipped by Tevinter and in the current timeline are sleeping, only to wake when darkspawn find them, at which point they transform into Archdemons and begin a Blight. The wiki describes them as such:

The Chantry teaches that when the Maker turned his back on spirits, some of them grew jealous of the living. Those powerful enough whispered to the living in dreams claiming that they were the true gods, the creators of the world and the living should bow down before them. Eventually, the living summoned them through the Veil. In the mortal realm these spirits took the form of dragons, winged Old Gods that ruled over the land. The living started to worship them instead of the Maker, an act recognized as the "Original Sin." In fury the Maker cursed the Old Gods, imprisoning them in underground tombs where they would slumber eternally.

Mike Searle Dragon Age: Origins Official Game Guide

While our guild cannot promise not to raise another Blight (we promise we will try not to destroy the world), we have taken the name of the Old Gods and fight in their name, mostly because it's pretty cool. For now, we rally our teams in unison against the other guilds.

Join

OG members, please join the palringo group. You will need to use the menu option Groups->Join a group and then enter hoda |og|. We had to remove the group from being searchable because of spambots flooding the chat.

To join for guild wars, join Palringo or post in the forum page. Provide your ally code, IGN, and forum name so that we are able to include you in our teams. There is no need to change your IGN, though if you wish, our guild tag is |OG|. Use lowercase L if inputting it in yourself.
